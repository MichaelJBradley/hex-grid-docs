readonly FloatHex[] ToWorldMatrix
 The matrix to convert from Hex coordinates to world coordinates. It has a length of 2 where index 0 is used to calculate x and 1 to calculate z. The choice to use FloatHex[] is fairly arbitrary. There may be a better choice, but it made sense to multiply the Q's and R's of the Hexes together.
 
readonly Vector3[] ToHexMatrix
 The matrix to convert from world coordinates to Hex coordinates. It has a length of 2 where index 0 is used to calculate Q and 1 to calculate R. The choice to use Vector3[] is fairly arbitrary. There may be a better choice, but it made sense to multiply the x's and z's of the Vector3s together.
